Features

Everything a young reader needs

Peregrine combines speech recognition, AI-generated stories, and a patient tutor to create a reading experience that grows with your child.

🎤

Voice-Powered Reading

Students read aloud and Peregrine listens in real time, tracking each word as they go. Words turn green as they read, giving instant positive reinforcement.

🧠

AI Reading Tutor

A patient, spoken tutor waits quietly while the student reads. When they pause or get stuck on a word, the tutor gently helps with pronunciation and encouragement.

📚

Grade-Level Stories

AI-generated reading content is tailored to each grade level using research-backed reading standards, from simple sight words in Grade 1 to complex narratives in Grade 6.

📊

Accuracy Tracking

Every reading session tracks accuracy and words-per-minute. Students and parents can see progress over time and identify areas for improvement.

📖

Personalized Library

Students build their own library of books across topics they love. Each book is generated at their reading level with multiple chapters to keep them engaged.

🏆

Built for Kids

A friendly, encouraging interface designed for elementary students. No red marks or harsh corrections, just green words and a helpful tutor voice.
